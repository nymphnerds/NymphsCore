// Features

Built for world
builders.

From rich text editing to AI tools, relationship graphs, and timeline views — WORBI packs the workflow of a full world-building studio into one app.

✍️
Rich Text Editor
Powered by TipTap (ProseMirror) for a fast, familiar writing experience with full formatting control.
🔗
WikiLinks
Link documents together with [[name]] syntax for cross-references across your world bible.
📅
Timeline View
Visualize your story chronologically with an integrated timeline for events and quests.
🕸️
Relationship Graph
Force-directed graph visualization of connections between characters, factions, locations, and more.
Cytoscape.js
🤖
AI Chat Panel
Built-in AI assistant with 8 tools for writing help, brainstorming, and document generation.
16+ LLM providers
📋
Document Templates
Start with templates for characters, quests, locations, items, factions, creatures, and timelines.
🏷️
Tag System
Organize documents with tags, metadata, and searchable relationships between entries.
⏰
Reminders
Set reminders for plot points, deadlines, or recurring events in your project.
🖼️
Image Generation
Generate images for your world using the integrated Z-Image AI generation pipeline.
📦
Game File Export
Export your documents as game-ready files for engines and pipelines.
📖
Story Bible Compiler
Compile your world documents into a single story bible for sharing or publishing.
🎨
Themes & Profiles
Customize your workspace with theme settings and workspace profiles for game or work modes.

Your world.One workspace.

WORBI brings together rich text editing, AI tools, relationship tracking, and timeline views in a single app built for world builders.
